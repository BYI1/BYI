COVID-19 Donations

With the remaining funds from previous projects, as well as other donations, BYI was able to help three organizations: Parikrama, Pratyush, and Doctors Without Borders. BYI donated $300 to Parikrama to provide meals for families in need. We also donated to Pratyush to help feed the families of daily wage workers. Finally, BYI donated $500 to Doctors Without Borders to help with their COVID Relief Fund.

SCHOOL SUPPLIES DRIVE

ARDSI(Alzheimer's and Related Disorders Society Of India)'s vision is to create a unique place for people with memory loss due to dementia. AMPHAN CYCLONE FUND

In Hingalganj, houses are completely destroyed because of a cyclone. The people are living in an accommodation provided by the government. They will not be able to do any agricultural activities for the next 3 years. In addition, the large scale saline water flooding is causing various skin diseases for people. To help, BYI is helping villagers buy medicine.

SANKARA EYE FOUNDATION

The magnitude of blindness is huge. India has the largest population of the world’s blind; with over 45 million visually impaired and 12 million of them totally blind. Their mission is to reach out to the rural poor and provide quality eye care free of cost. Baybasi Youth Initiative fundraised for Sankara Eye Foundation during the 2016 Autumn Festival to help with 83 curable blindness cases in India.

About us

Baybasi, Inc. was incorporated in July 2008 as a non-profit charitable organization and is presently located in Foster City, California. The purpose of the corporation is to serve (without limitations), the Asian Indian community (people originating in the state of West Bengal, India and its neighboring states and countries speaking the language Bengali at home, and other Indian community members living in the Foster City, San Mateo, and the adjacent areas of the San Francisco Bay Area.

The BYI community is not just the Bengali population of the Bay Area, but all of the children, teens, adults, and seniors in need of a better world. Our vision is to help everyone, through a vast range of different projects, so that we can empower people to stand up and make a change in the world.
